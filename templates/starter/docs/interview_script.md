# Customer Discovery Interview Script

**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Target:** The user/persona defined in `plan/main_plan.md` after `/plan-loop`.

## Intro (1 min)

"I'm exploring a product for [target user]. I'm not selling today. I want to understand your workflow, pain, current tools, and whether this problem is worth solving."

## Questions

1. **Role:** What is your role and how often do you deal with this problem?
2. **Workflow:** Walk me through how you handle it today.
3. **Pain:** What is most painful, slow, expensive, or risky?
4. **Frequency:** How often does this happen?
5. **Current tools:** What tools, spreadsheets, vendors, or manual steps do you use?
6. **Workarounds:** What have you tried? Why did it fail or stick?
7. **Budget:** Who owns budget for solving this?
8. **Trust:** What would make you trust or reject a new product here?
9. **Data:** What data/files/events would the product need? Can synthetic examples be created?
10. **Pilot:** What would a useful first demo or pilot need to prove?
